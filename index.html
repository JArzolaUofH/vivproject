Meet Vivian!
Houston PMU Artist | Owner
Licensed & Certified in TX

Vivian's journey into the world of permanent makeup began in Fall 2021, after she enrolled in a PMU course that sparked an undeniable passion. This interest quickly evolved into a dedicated pursuit of mastery.

She spent over a year and a half under the mentorship of a seasoned professional, honing her skills and refining her artistry. In April 2023, she finally took a leap of faith to open up a small studio with her best friend.

Her commitment to excellence is evident in every set of brows she designs, ensuring each client receives a personalized experience that highlights their unique features and meets their individual preferences.

Vivian's mission extends beyond the creation of beautiful brows. She is dedicated to enhancing the lives of women by offering services that not only save time in their daily routines but also boost their confidence and self-esteem. She approaches each client with meticulous attention to detail and a heart full of compassion.

If you're looking to achieve your ideal brows and embrace a more confident, radiant self, Vivian is the artist you can trust.

General Policies

  • Previous Permanent Makeup

    In cases where the previous artist’s work is too dark, dense, discolored, or large, tattoo removal will highly be recommended before you can book.

  • Allergies

    Any allergies or skin conditions must be reported to the artist.
    A patch test can be administered prior to the appointment. If needed, please contact the artist to book.

  • Medical History

    Any medical conditions or current prescription medications must be reported to the artist to verify if the service/procedure is safe to proceed.

  • Client Responsibilites

    Please read and follow Pre-Care Instructions
    Failure to do so may result in contraindications on the day of the appointment, which could result in being sent home to reschedule a new appointment.
    Deposits will not be refunded due to the client’s failure to follow Pre-Care.
